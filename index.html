Doodle with ack-ack

play presentation
    Rules:
  • You shouldn't use the button "UP", because you get -100 points.
  • Each platform passed +10 points, platform with coin +50 points.
  • If you touch bottom you lose.
  • If the bullet hit you, then you lose -100 points.
  • You can only jump on the white platform twice, after it disappears.
  • If you jump on the platform with the trampoline, you will jump high.

Doodle with ack-ack is arcade game.The main aim of the game is help Doodle to get as high as possible on the platforms and score many points.

  • jump to left
  • jump to right
  • jump to up
